I am an actor and educator based in Chicago, IL. When not performing, I can be found tutoring Language Arts, acting out my Jeopardy! contestant dreams in my living room, singing along to Dolly Parton, hiking, or reading the latest horror novel I’ve gotten my hands on.

I finally stumbled into a love of performing after getting past my first few tragic, preteen-awkwardness-fueled shows; since then, it’s been full steam ahead in my mission to craft a career in the arts that is equal parts empowering and joyful. And, it is that empowerment and joy that I receive from the arts that I wish to radiate outwards.

After graduating with a degree in Theatre-Acting from Illinois State University in 2019, I continued developing my skills not only as a performing artist, but as an individual with a passion for learning, teaching, and cultivating creativity through various mediums and practices.
